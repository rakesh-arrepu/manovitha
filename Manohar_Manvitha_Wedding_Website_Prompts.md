**Wedding Date:** May 3, 2026\
**Location:** South Jordan, Utah\
**Theme:** Love \| Indian Aesthetic \| Lavender & White \| Rich &
Elegant

## 1️⃣ Full Wedding Landing Page Prompt (Rich & Royal Indian Aesthetic)

Create a premium wedding website landing page for Manohar & Manvitha
with a romantic love theme and elegant Indian aesthetic.\
Use a lavender and white color palette with soft gradients and floral
textures.\
The design should feel rich, graceful, and culturally rooted but
modern.\
Include subtle Indian elements like lotus motifs, mandala patterns,
jasmine flowers, silk textures, gold foil accents, and temple-style
typography.

**Sections required:**\
- Hero section with couple names "Manohar & Manvitha" in elegant
calligraphy\
- Wedding date "May 3, 2026" with countdown timer\
- Our Love Story timeline section\
- Wedding Events (Haldi, Mehendi, Sangeet, Wedding Ceremony, Reception)\
- Venue details in South Jordan, Utah with map integration\
- Photo gallery with soft glow effect\
- RSVP form\
- Footer with family names and blessings message

Add floating floral animations, soft background music option, and smooth
scrolling transitions.
